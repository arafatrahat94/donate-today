Our Donate Plan

Some initial plans of our organization shown below. We are working on adding more and more targets to achive them with our dignity. Share your ideas with us if they are not listed below.

Food for a family

We are working on looking for families who are starving around us. We are arranging quality food for them once in a week and distribute them around the city. We are trying to spread this activities as much as possible across the city and villages.

Shelter for a family

We are looking for poeple living on footpaths around us and providing them money to build at least a home where they can live with their families. We are also working provide simple jobs for them so they can earn money and feed their family members.

Education for a child

So many children around us are not having proper education due to financial crisis. We are trying our best to help them with money so that they can go to school and reduce child-labor tendency. Todays kid is the future of our country.
